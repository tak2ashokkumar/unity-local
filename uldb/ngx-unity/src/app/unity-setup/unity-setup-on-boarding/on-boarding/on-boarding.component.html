UNITYOne.AI supports two types of configuration to connect UNITYOne.AI to your infrastructure i.e with & without Collector. In the default Configuration, Connectivity needs to be established via IPSec VPN tunnel. And for the Collector Configuration, a VM to be installed at customer subnet to deploy the UNITY Collector packages and we will need a 1:1 NAT mapping to give the collector VM a public IP for Unity SaaS to talk directly.

In this phase, you can perform auto discovery of devices in your network. Also download, fill and upload the Onboarding Template with all the details updated.

In this section you can enable monitoring of all your onboarded devices provided SNMP is configured. Please contact support@unityonecloud.com for further assistance with SNMP configuration. Also download, update & upload the VM lists from respective clouds to enable Monitoring of VMs.
